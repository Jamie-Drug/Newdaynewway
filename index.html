Alternative Learning in Norfolk's Therapeutic Environment

What we do

New Day New Way is an alternative provision for children and young people needing an alternative way of learning to main stream school. It provides a one to one therapeutic environment looking after animals and learning how to grow veg.

Paddle board and kayak around Norfolk with a qualified Paddle sport instructor and hike through Norfolk countryside

Experience foraging and cooking to develop important life skills.

New Day New Way offer termly incentives including high ropes at Thetford or a segway tour through the forest.

More about us

New Day New Way would suit young people with an adventurous nature, who love being outside and exploring the beauty of Norfolk and all it has to offer.
