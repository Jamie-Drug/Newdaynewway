About New Day New Way

Therapeutic alternative learning through adventure, nature, and animal care in beautiful Norfolk.

Our Story

Hello, I'm Louise Dowle-Moore. I've been a foster carer since 2005, supporting some of Norfolk’s most vulnerable young people.

I created New Day New Way to offer a one-to-one therapeutic alternative for those who struggle in mainstream education — especially those with challenging behaviour or at risk of exclusion.

Through outdoor adventures and hands-on animal care, we help young people re-engage with learning in a calm, safe, and exciting environment. They gain new skills and can work towards paddlesport certificates and the John Muir Award.

Who We Support

  • Adventurous young people who love the outdoors
  • Those who thrive with one-to-one support
  • Children who find mainstream school difficult
